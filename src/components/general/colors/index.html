Colors

There are three primary brand colors that are derived from the logo. These are mostly used as accents like red headings are strongly Mapzen.

Red ($mz-red)

Blue ($mz-blue)

Yellow ($mz-yellow)

Secondary colors complement the colorful primary palette and used for text, borders etc.

Off Black ($mz-black)

Dark Gray ($mz-darkgray)

Medium Gray ($mz-mediumgray)

Background Gray ($mz-lightgray)

Tertiary colors are derived from the primary colors and are used in rare cases like as hover states for buttons

Dark Red ($mz-darkred)

Dark Blue ($mz-darkblue)

Dark Yellow ($mz-darkyellow)

Brown ($mz-brown)
