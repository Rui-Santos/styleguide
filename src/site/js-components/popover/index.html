Popover

Popovers add small overlays of content to any element for housing secondary information.

Toggle popover on left | Toggle popover on top | Toggle popover on left

Popovers distort the original elements margin to display themselves causing jittering. To prevent this, we had to get rid of margin of the button when the button is used with Popover. In result, when you use .btn-group wrapper for the series of buttons, you will see the buttons leaving no space for each other.

When you need to use a series of buttons with Popover, please use buttons without .btn-group wrapper like below.
